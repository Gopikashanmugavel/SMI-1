Welcome to Spice Masters International

Premium Cardamom & Organic Spice Exporters

Spice Masters International (SMI) is a leading manufacturer, producer, and exporter of Indian Green Cardamom, based in Bodinayakanur, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 2016, SMI has built a reputation for delivering premium-quality cardamom, sourced from the finest plantations in Idukki, Kerala, and Spices Board India auctions.
