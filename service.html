Common Questions & Answers

Spice Masters International (SMI) sources its Green Cardamom from the finest plantations in Idukki, Kerala, and Spices Board India auctions. Each batch undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure superior size, color, aroma, and purity, making it the preferred choice for food manufacturers and spice traders.

SMI follows a strict sourcing process, procuring cardamom directly from trusted farmers, plantations, and Spices Board auctions. The quality control team ensures that only the best cardamom—meeting size, aroma, and purity standards—enters the supply chain, maintaining consistency and excellence in every batch.

SMI supplies premium cardamom to leading food manufacturers, including Aachi Masala (Chennai), Priya Masala (Hyderabad), and Swastik Masala (Bangalore). The company also serves spice traders across major Indian cities like Mumbai, Ahmedabad, and Delhi, along with expanding its presence on B2B platforms like Amazon and Indiamart.

SMI prioritizes sustainable sourcing and responsible agricultural practices to support the environment. Additionally, the company actively empowers women by employing around 25 women in its processing unit, providing stable livelihoods and skill development opportunities.
